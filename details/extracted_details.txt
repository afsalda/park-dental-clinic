 ➔   HOMEPAGE INTRO 
 At Park Dental Clinic, we believe every patient deserves a dentist who listens as 
 well as they treat. Located in the heart of Elamakkara, Kochi, we bring together 
 specialist-level expertise, modern equipment, and a genuinely warm clinic environment 
 — so that walking in feels less like a medical visit and more like being taken care of. 
 ➔   ABOUT US SECTION 
 Park Dental Clinic was founded on a simple belief — that quality dental care should 
 feel approachable, not intimidating. 
 Led by Dr. Monish, a Dental Surgeon and Implantologist with over 20 years of 
 experience, the clinic has grown into one of Elamakkara's most trusted multispeciality dental 
 practices. Dr. Monish completed his BDS from Rajas Dental College and his postgraduate 
 degree from Manipal University, and is a member of the Indian Dental Association. 
 From routine cleanings to advanced smile makeovers, from painless root canals to precision 
 implant placements, every procedure at Park Dental is performed with the same standard — 
 modern equipment, B-class sterilisation, and a team that takes the time to explain every step 
 before it happens. 
 Patients have travelled from across Kerala — and even from Lakshadweep — to 
 receive care here. With a 4.9 rating across nearly 900 patient reviews, the clinic's reputation 
 speaks for itself. But behind the numbers is something simpler: a team that genuinely cares 
 about the people walking through the door. 
 Whether it's your first dental visit or your tenth, you're in good hands at Park Dental.
